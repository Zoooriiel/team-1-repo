About Root Squad

Welcome to Root Squad, the cozy corner for house plant lovers! Whether you're a seasoned plant parent or just starting your green journey, Root Squad is the perfect place to connect with fellow plant enthusiasts. Our app brings together people who share a passion for house plants, allowing you to swap tips, share your plant stories, and discover new leafy friends. With features like plant care advice and a lively community forum, Root Squad is all about growing together. Join us and be part of a warm, welcoming community that celebrates the joy of house plants!
